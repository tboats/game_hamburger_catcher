# Template: Email (Professional Communication)

> For professional emails: outreach, proposals, follow-ups, announcements.
> Target length: 20–80 lines.

## Standard Structure

```markdown
# Email: [Subject Line]

> **Version:** X.Y.Z | **Updated:** YYYY-MM-DD
> **To:** [Recipient name / role / organization]
> **From:** [Sender identity]
> **Purpose:** [outreach | proposal | follow-up | announcement | inquiry]

---

## Context

[1-2 sentences: Why are you writing this email? What is the relationship?]

---

## Email Content

**Subject:** [Clear, specific subject line — max 60 characters]

[Greeting — formal or semi-formal based on relationship]

[Opening paragraph — 2-3 sentences establishing purpose and context]

[Body paragraph(s) — key information, value proposition, or request]

[Call-to-Action — one clear, specific next step]

[Closing — professional sign-off]

---

## Metadata

| Field       | Value                                   |
|:------------|:----------------------------------------|
| Tone        | [formal / semi-formal / friendly]       |
| Language    | [configured language + English terms]   |
| Length      | [short: <100 words / standard: 100-250 / detailed: 250+] |
| Follow-up   | [date or condition for follow-up]       |
```

## Email Categories

| Category      | Use case                                  | Tone default    |
|:------------- |:----------------------------------------- |:--------------- |
| `outreach`    | First contact, cold email, introduction   | Semi-formal     |
| `proposal`    | Business proposal, partnership offer      | Formal          |
| `follow-up`   | After meeting, after proposal, reminder   | Semi-formal     |
| `announcement`| Product launch, feature update, news      | Formal          |
| `inquiry`     | Asking for information, support request   | Semi-formal     |
| `thank-you`   | Appreciation after event, collaboration   | Friendly        |

## Additional Rules

1. **Subject line:** MUST be clear, specific, and under 60 characters.
2. **Call-to-Action:** Every email MUST have exactly ONE clear CTA.
3. **Length:** Keep emails concise. Default max: 250 words unless `--depth exhaustive`.
4. **No fluff:** Remove filler phrases ("I hope this email finds you well").
5. **Professional sign-off:** Match tone to email category.
6. **Source reference:** Header MUST contain recipient context and purpose.
